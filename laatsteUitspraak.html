Michael Slager sentenced to death penalty

Today, December 7, new information has been revealed about the incident with Walter Scott. U.S. District Judge David C. Norton sentenced officer Michael Slager with the death penalty for manslaughter. Although prosecutors had argued for voluntary manslaughter, the judge agreed with attorneys that the "inappropriate underlying offense" was aggravated first-degree murder. An appeal for reduction of sentence has been denied.
